# Privacy Policy for Richfield Area Chamber of Commerce Mobile App

**Last Updated: January 15, 2025**

## Introduction

The Richfield Area Chamber of Commerce ("RACC", "we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Account Information**: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, business name, and membership information
- **Profile Information**: Business details, contact information, and professional information you choose to share
- **Communications**: Messages and correspondence you send through the App
- **Nominations and Submissions**: Information you provide when nominating businesses or individuals for awards

### Information Automatically Collected
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, and mobile device identifiers
- **Usage Data**: How you interact with the App, features used, and time spent
- **Location Information**: General geographic location based on IP address (we do not collect precise GPS location)

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Provide and maintain the App's functionality
- Manage your membership account
- Facilitate networking and connections between members
- Notify you about Chamber events and updates
- Process nominations and award submissions
- Improve the App and user experience
- Send important updates and announcements
- Respond to your inquiries and support requests

## Information Sharing and Disclosure

### Member Directory
Member business information is shared within the Member Directory feature, visible only to other RACC members. This includes:
- Business name and description
- Contact information
- Industry/category
- Website and social media links

### We Do Not Sell Your Information
We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

### Service Providers
We may share information with trusted service providers who assist us in operating the App, including:
- Cloud hosting services
- Analytics providers
- Communication services

### Legal Requirements
We may disclose information if required by law or in response to valid legal processes.

## Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

## Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:
- Access your personal information
- Update or correct your information
- Request deletion of your account and data
- Opt-out of non-essential communications
- Restrict certain data processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at [your-email@raccmn.com]

## Data Retention

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information we collect and the right to request deletion.
