# Google Play Store Release Notes

## Version 1.0.0 - Initial Release

### What's New

Welcome to the official Richfield Area Chamber of Commerce mobile app! This is our first release bringing the Chamber experience directly to your Android device.

**Features:**

📅 **Event Calendar**
• Browse upcoming Chamber events and networking opportunities
• View event details and RSVP directly from the app
• Add events to your device calendar

👥 **Member Directory**
• Access the complete RACC member directory
• Search and filter businesses by category
• Connect with fellow Chamber members

🏆 **Awards & Recognition**
• View Business of the Month highlights
• See Customer Service Superstar winners
• Celebrate Business of the Year achievements

📰 **News & Updates**
• Stay informed with the latest Chamber announcements
• Read important business news and resources

👤 **Member Profile**
• Manage your membership information
• Update your business details
• Access member-only content

🎯 **Nominations**
• Submit Business of the Month nominations
• Nominate outstanding customer service representatives
• Participate in annual award selections

**User Experience:**
• Beautiful dark mode optimized for mobile viewing
• Smooth animations and intuitive navigation
• Responsive design for all Android devices
• Fast performance and offline capability

Download now and stay connected with the Richfield Area Chamber of Commerce community!
