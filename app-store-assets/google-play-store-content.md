## App Name

## Short Description (80 characters max)

## Full Description (4000 characters max)

Stay connected with the Richfield Area Chamber of Commerce (RACC) community through our official mobile app. Built for members and the local business community, this app provides seamless access to everything you need to engage with the Chamber.

**KEY FEATURES:**

📅 **Event Calendar**
• View upcoming Chamber events, mixers, and networking opportunities
• Get event details, dates, times, and locations
• RSVP directly from your phone
• Add events to your device calendar

👥 **Member Directory**
• Browse the complete RACC member directory
• Search for businesses by name, category, or industry
• View member profiles with contact information
• Connect directly with other Chamber members

🏆 **Awards & Recognition**
• Business of the Month highlights
• Customer Service Superstar recognition
• Business of the Year celebrations
• View award winners and their achievements

📰 **News & Updates**
• Stay informed about Chamber announcements
• Read the latest business news affecting Richfield
• Get notified about important updates
• Access resources and support for local businesses

👤 **Member Profile**
• Manage your membership information
• Update your business details
• View your membership status
• Access member-only resources

🎯 **Nominations**
• Submit nominations for Business of the Month
• Nominate outstanding customer service representatives
• Participate in annual award selections
• Celebrate excellence in the community

**ABOUT RACC:**

The Richfield Area Chamber of Commerce serves as the voice of business in Richfield, Minnesota. We support local businesses through networking opportunities, advocacy, education, and community engagement. Our mission is to promote economic growth and enhance the quality of life in the Richfield area.

**WHO IS THIS APP FOR:**

• RACC Members - Full access to all features
• Local Businesses - Stay connected with the community
• Community Members - Discover local businesses and events
• Networking Professionals - Connect with business leaders

**BENEFITS:**

✓ Never miss a Chamber event
✓ Easy access to member businesses
✓ Stay updated on community news
✓ Network on-the-go
✓ Support local businesses
✓ Participate in member recognition

Download now and become more engaged with the Richfield Area Chamber of Commerce community!
